# One-Minute Scalping Strategy

##### The Checklist: [ref] --> [One Minute Strategy Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_JqoMJIU-co)

### Checklist

1.  Heiken Ashi chart
2.  100 EMA on chart
3.  Market Structure
    *   Above EMA: Buys above EMA
    *   Below EMA: Sells below EMA
4.  Clean pullback:
    *   At least 2 clean opposite color candles (in pullback direction)
    *   (“Clean = no wicks on the top for buy; no wicks on the bottom for sells)
5.  Enter on High volume ‘Doji’ (as soon as candle closes)
    *   Entry candlestick must be a ‘Doji’, preferably in the direction of the trade.
    *   High volume = larger or the same size as the last 1-3 candles
    *   Preferable if the ‘Doji’ is in the direction of the trade direction (e.g., red for a sale)
6.  Stop Loss (SL):
    *   SELL -> Place SL Above ‘Doji’
    *   BUY -> Place SL Below ‘Doji’
7.  Take Profit (TP):
    *   1:2 Risk-To-Reward Ratio (Draw entry box at wick of entry candle regardless if you have a better entry)
    *   You can go higher (e.g., 1:2), but the win rate will drop

### Vocabulary

**Doji:**
A single candlestick pattern in which the open and close prices of the security or market are the same or very close to it. Doji form when the open and close of a candlestick are equal, or very close to equal.


*Reference:* [Investopedia - Doji](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/d/doji.asp)

*Reference:* [Forex.com - Doji Candle](https://www.forex.com/en-us/learn-fo%20inverted%20cross.)

**EMA (Exponential Moving Average):**
An exponential moving average (EMA) is a type of moving average (MA) that places a greater weight and significance on the most recent data points. Also known as the exponentially weighted moving average.
